Community Services

MIA is committed to providing a wide range of services to support the diverse needs of the Muslim community in Manitoba, fostering well-being and mutual support.

Funeral Services (Janazah)

We provide support and guidance for funeral arrangements according to Islamic traditions. Our team assists families during their time of need with compassion and care.

Learn More

Marriage Services

MIA offers services related to Islamic marriage contracts (Nikah), including officiating ceremonies and providing pre-marital counseling.

Details & Booking

Mental Health & Wellness

Confidential counselling services and resources to support individuals and families facing challenges, provided with cultural sensitivity.

Resources & Contact

Financial Assistance (Takaful)

Through our Takaful program, we provide financial assistance to community members facing hardship, based on mutual support and Islamic charity.

New Muslim Support

Dedicated support and resources for individuals new to Islam, helping them integrate into the community and learn about their faith.

Outreach Programs

MIA actively engages in outreach programs to build bridges with the wider community, promote interfaith dialogue, and share information about Islam.
