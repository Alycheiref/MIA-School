About Us

Over the past few years, the Muslim community has grown significantly. There has been an influx of Muslims from all over the world and we look forward to supporting our community with an Islamic community school based on the recommendations of our community.

MIA Institute of Knowledge aims to provide an Islamic-centered education for students starting in Kindergarten through Grades 6. We believe that Allah (SWT) is the source of knowledge and all the streams of knowledge guide us to oneness of Allah (SWT). The MIA Institute of Knowledge strives to provide quality education based around Islamic values, by embedding Islamic concepts through the subjects being taught. In our classroom, we will focus on developing moral values alongside a strong academic curriculum. Our goal is for children to want Islam, love Islam and live Islam.

Vision, Mission & Objectives

Vision

To provide a safe space for students to learn and grow as proud Muslim Canadian citizens.

Mission

To provide quality education based on Islamic values and develop leaders of tomorrow.

Objectives

  • To empower students to meet challenges and pursue goals.
  • To impart knowledge to prepare Muslim leaders.
  • To encourage development of social responsibility.
